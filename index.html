What is a zachathon?

A zackathon is a place where the only thing limiting what you build is your own creativity. Stay up for 18+ hours with the smartest people you'll ever meet, building the coolest things you have created and chugging more Red Bull than you ever thought possible.

When should I arrive at the venue?

The opening ceremony starts at 8pm on Saturday, March 22th. Be sure to find a place to stay if you plan on arriving before January 24th! There are plenty of cool places to check out if you get to El Segundo early.
